# Exercise Image Generation: Sample Prompts (Line Art Style)

Based on your preference for "clean line art" that shows equipment, action, and muscle groups, here are a few sample prompts to test the style.

## 1. Barbell Back Squat (槓鈴深蹲)
- **Concept**: Show the user in the bottom position of a squat with a barbell on their shoulders.
- **Prompt**: `Minimalist vector line art of a person performing a barbell back squat. Side view. Black lines on white background. Focus on posture and equipment (barbell). Highlight the primary muscle groups (Quadriceps, Glutes) with subtle shading or thicker lines. No text. Clean, professional medical illustration style.`

## 2. Push-up (伏地挺身)
- **Concept**: Person in a plank position, mid-descent.
- **Prompt**: `Clean line art illustration of a person doing a push-up. Isometric view. Stroke weight balance. Clear depiction of hands on floor and straight body line. Highlight chest and triceps muscles. White background, black ink style, no text.`

## 3. Lat Pulldown (滑輪下拉)
- **Concept**: Seated at a machine, pulling the bar down to the chest.
- **Prompt**: `Minimalist line drawing of a fitness exercise: Seated Lat Pulldown. Technical illustration style. Show the cable machine and the bar. Highlight the back muscles (Latissimus Dorsi). Black and white, high contrast, clean paths, no text.`

## 4. Plank (棒式)
- **Concept**: Static core exercise on forearms.
- **Prompt**: `Single line style or minimalist vector art of a plank exercise. Showcase the core tension. Profile view. Simple, elegant lines. Clear visual of the floor and body alignment. No text.`
